Homework on a Snow Day

Doing homework on snowdays is tough. On one hand I just want to rekax and enjoy the day off. On the other hand I try to motivate myself and get some work done. One thing that works for me personally is doing homework in short segments. So I work on hw for an hour and take a 30 minute break and continue that process for as long as I can. Or if I'm really motivated I try and knock it all out in one sitting and finish it as soon as possible so I can enjoy the day.

The Polar Vortex is why we have all these days off school.

When the Polar Vortex is over we will be back in class regularly
